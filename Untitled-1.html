Ingredients

Steak

A delicious grilled steak, cooked to perfection.

Dish: Steak with Garlic Butter
Ingredients: Steak, garlic, butter, herbs
Instructions: Grill the steak and top with garlic butter.

Pasta

Freshly made pasta with a variety of sauces to choose from.

Dish: Pesto Pasta
Ingredients: Pasta, pesto, parmesan, olive oil
Instructions: Toss cooked pasta with pesto sauce and top with cheese.

Fish

A savory fish dish with spices and herbs.

Dish: Lemon Herb Fish
Ingredients: Fish, lemon, herbs, olive oil
Instructions: Bake fish with lemon slices and herbs.

Doro Wot

A traditional Ethiopian chicken stew served with injera.

Dish: Doro Wot
Ingredients: Chicken, berbere spice, onions, butter
Instructions: Simmer chicken in a rich berbere sauce.

Kitfo

A flavorful Ethiopian dish made from minced raw beef, seasoned with spices.

Dish: Kitfo with Injera
Ingredients: Minced beef, mitmita, butter
Instructions: Mix raw beef with spices and clarified butter.

Chicken

Succulent roasted chicken, perfectly seasoned.

Dish: Roast Chicken
Ingredients: Chicken, garlic, rosemary, olive oil
Instructions: Roast chicken with herbs and garlic.
